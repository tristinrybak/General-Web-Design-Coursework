Image Types

JPEG (Joint Photographic Experts Group)

A JPEG is best for photographic images and provides a compressed format that gives a small file size when faced with lots of color and variation.

GIF (Graphic Interchange Format)

A GIF is best for limited colors and allows for threshhold transparency and animation.

PNG (Portable Network Graphics)

A PNG does well with a limited color and photographic content. It allows for gradient and subtle transparency.
