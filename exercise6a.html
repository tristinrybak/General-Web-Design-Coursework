Lists

Heirarchy structuring of content
(Also useful for navigation.)

Definition Lists

The simplest list type.

Doe
a deer, a female deer
Ray
a drop of golden sun
Me
a name I call myself

Ordered Lists

These lists use numbers to form outlines.
(The kind of numeral is controlled with CSS.)

  1. Needs
  2. Wants

You can tell it what number you want it what number to start on:

  1. School
  2. World Travel
  3. Job (definitely!)

You can force an order with specific values:

  1. Bottles of beer on the wall. . .
  2. Bottles of beer on the wall. . .
  3. Bottles of beer on the wall. . .

Lists can be multilayered:

  1. Friends
  2. Family
    1. Parents
      1. Mom
      2. Dad
    2. Sisters
      1. Kourtney
      2. Kim
      3. Khloe

Unordered Lists

These Lists dont use numbers to form outlines.
(The kind of bullet is controlled with CSS.)

  • Good
  • Bad
  • Ugly

Lists can be multilayered:

  • Friends
  • Family
    • Parents
      • Mom
      • Dad
    • Sisters
      • Kourtney
      • Kim
      • Khloe
